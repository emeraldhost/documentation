# Arma Reforger

## What is Arma Reforger?

Arma Reforger is a military tactical simulation developed by Bohemia Interactive. As the first title built on the new Enfusion engine, the game serves both as a technical foundation and bridge title to future Arma games. It offers realistic, ground-based combat set during the Cold War on the fictional island of Everon.

At the heart of Arma Reforger are realism, tactical teamplay and large, open battlefields. Players use a wide selection of weapons, vehicles and equipment from the late 1980s era. The Conflict game mode pits two factions against each other in a dynamic, large-scale capture-and-hold battle, while Game Master enables creative scenarios led by a game director.

The game offers extensive modding capabilities through the integrated Workbench editor and Workshop integration. Dedicated servers support up to 128 players and allow hosting custom modes, mods and scenarios. Arma Reforger is cross-platform, connecting PC and console players on shared servers.

Arma Reforger is aimed at fans of realistic military simulations who value tactical depth, teamplay and creative freedom. The game is available on PC and consoles.
