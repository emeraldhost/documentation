# 7 Days to Die

## What is 7 Days to Die?

7 Days to Die is an open-world survival horror game developed by The Fun Pimps. The game combines elements of first-person shooter, survival horror, tower defense and role-playing game in a destructible voxel world. Players must fight to survive in a post-apocalyptic world after a nuclear catastrophe, where zombies have nearly wiped out humanity.

At the core of the game are resource gathering, crafting tools, weapons and equipment, and building a secure base. Every seventh day brings a so-called Blood Moon Horde, during which large numbers of zombies specifically attack the players' base and test their defenses.

The world of 7 Days to Die is fully destructible and modifiable. Players can tear down buildings, construct their own structures and explore various biomes ranging from deserts to snowy regions. The game supports co-op multiplayer with multiple players on a shared server.

7 Days to Die is particularly popular among fans of survival crafting games with a zombie theme who appreciate a combination of base building, resource management and intense combat. The game is available on PC via Steam.
