# Core Keeper

## What is Core Keeper?

Core Keeper is a top-down sandbox survival game developed by Swedish studio Pugstorm and published by Fireshine Games. The game is set in a procedurally generated underground world where players dig, explore, craft and fight.

Players begin in a central chamber around the eponymous Core and expand their world by digging through various biomes. Each biome brings new resources, enemies and environmental hazards. Defeating bosses unlocks new technologies and areas.

The gameplay combines elements of mining, crafting, farming, automation and combat. Players gather resources, build their base, craft advanced tools and weapons, and uncover the secrets of the underground world.

Core Keeper supports both singleplayer and cooperative multiplayer for up to 8 players. The game is available on PC (Steam), PlayStation, Xbox and Nintendo Switch.
