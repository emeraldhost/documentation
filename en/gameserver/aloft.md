# Aloft

## What is Aloft?

Aloft is a cooperative survival and exploration game set in a world of flying islands, developed by Astrolabe Interactive. Players navigate a sky-bound world where living island creatures drift through the clouds, and must confront a mysterious fungal corruption that threatens the nature of the islands.

At the core of the game are sailing, exploration, crafting and base building. Players turn islands into their mobile ships by raising sails and travel through the sky aboard them. At the same time, they tend to nature, cultivate resources and uncover the secrets of the living world to push back the corruption.

The game world features handcrafted biomes, dynamic weather systems and diverse wildlife that players can discover and interact with. Aloft supports solo play as well as co-op multiplayer with up to eight players on a shared server.

Aloft is particularly popular among players who enjoy a relaxed, atmospheric survival experience focused on exploration, nature shaping and co-op play. The game is available on PC via Steam.
