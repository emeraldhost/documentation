# How to Find Out Your SteamID64

:::: info Info
When you enter your `Steam username`, multiple accounts may be displayed. Select your account.
::::

1. <b>Open SteamID website</b><br>
   Go to the [SteamID](https://steamid.io/) website.

2. <b>Enter your details</b><br>
   Enter your `Steam username` or the `Steam profile URL`.

3. <b>Look up SteamID64</b><br>
   Click on "Lookup", then your `SteamID64` will be displayed.
