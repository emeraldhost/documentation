# DayZ

## What is DayZ?

DayZ is an open-world survival game developed by Bohemia Interactive. The game takes place in a post-apocalyptic world where players must fight for survival in the fictional post-Soviet Republic of Chernarus.

In DayZ, players must find food, water, and equipment while defending themselves against zombies and other players. The game is known for its realistic survival approach, where diseases, injuries, and weather play an important role.

Dedicated servers allow players to create their own communities with custom rules and mods. The active modding community offers numerous extensions that customize and expand the gaming experience.
