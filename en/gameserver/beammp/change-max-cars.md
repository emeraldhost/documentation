# How to Change the Maximum Cars on Your BeamMP Server

You can configure how many cars each player is allowed to spawn at the same time.

1. <b>Open dashboard</b><br>
   Open the dashboard of your server.

2. <b>Open settings</b><br>
   Navigate to the **Settings**.

3. <b>Set max cars</b><br>
   Enter the desired number in the **Maximum Cars** field.

4. <b>Restart the server</b><br>
   Save the setting and restart your server.
