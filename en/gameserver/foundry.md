# FOUNDRY

## What is FOUNDRY?

FOUNDRY is a first-person factory building game developed by Channel 3 Entertainment and published by Paradox Interactive. The game combines classic factory-building mechanics with a fully destructible voxel-based world — similar to Minecraft but focused on automation and industrial construction.

At the core of FOUNDRY is the automation of production chains. Players build conveyor belts, machines and factories to turn raw materials into increasingly complex products. The world can be freely modified — flattening mountains, digging tunnels, shaping custom landscapes.

The game world features procedurally generated biomes with different resources and terrains. FOUNDRY supports solo play as well as co-op multiplayer, where several players can work together on massive factory complexes.

FOUNDRY is aimed at fans of automation and building games who want to combine creative freedom with voxel-based world shaping. The game is in Early Access and is available on PC via Steam.
