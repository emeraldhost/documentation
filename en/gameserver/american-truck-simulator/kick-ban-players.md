# How to Kick and Ban Players on Your American Truck Simulator Server

You can remove players from your session via the in-game convoy menu.

## Kick players via the convoy menu

1. <b>Join the server</b><br>
   Join your American Truck Simulator server.

2. <b>Open the convoy menu</b><br>
   Open the convoy menu in the game.

3. <b>Select a player</b><br>
   Select the player you want to remove.

4. <b>Kick the player</b><br>
   Click **Kick** to remove the player from the session.

:::: info Note
A kicked player can rejoin the session. There is no permanent ban feature in convoy mode.
::::
