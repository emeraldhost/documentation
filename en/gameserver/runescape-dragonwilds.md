# RuneScape: Dragonwilds

## What is RuneScape: Dragonwilds?

RuneScape: Dragonwilds is a cooperative survival crafting game set in the RuneScape universe, developed by Jagex using Unreal Engine 5. The game takes place on the forgotten continent of Ashenfall, where dragons have awoken and players must fight to survive as stranded survivors.

Players gather resources, build shelters, craft weapons and face the growing dragon threat. By mastering survival skills and unlocking ancient secrets, players can slay the Dragon Queen and reclaim Ashenfall — alone or with up to 5 friends.

The game combines the well-known RuneScape skills with survival mechanics, including wild, transformative magic, a robust building system, challenging combat and dungeons to explore. The hand-crafted game world is infused with magical energy and offers diverse regions.

RuneScape: Dragonwilds is available on PC via Steam.
