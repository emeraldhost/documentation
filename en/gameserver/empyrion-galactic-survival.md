# Empyrion: Galactic Survival

## What is Empyrion: Galactic Survival?

Empyrion: Galactic Survival is an open-world sandbox-survival game set in space, developed by Eleon Game Studios. The game combines spaceship construction, base building, role-playing elements and survival mechanics in a huge galaxy full of different planets, moons and space sectors. Players experience the freedom of designing their own ships and travelling the stars with them.

At the core of the game are building, exploration and combat: players construct ships and bases block by block, mine resources on planets, research new technologies and face off against hostile aliens, pirates and wild creatures. The different planets offer their own biomes, atmospheres and difficulty levels – from habitable worlds to deadly lava planets.

Empyrion supports both solo play and large multiplayer sessions. Everything from small co-op groups up to large communities running their own servers is possible. Mod support, scenarios and extensive configuration options enable very different play styles – from relaxed PvE to hardcore PvP.

Empyrion: Galactic Survival is aimed at players who want to combine creative freedom, spaceship construction and deep survival mechanics in a single game. The game is available on PC via Steam.
