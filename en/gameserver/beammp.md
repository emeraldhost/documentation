# BeamMP

## What is BeamMP?

BeamMP is a multiplayer modification for the vehicle simulation BeamNG.drive, developed by the BeamMP community. While the original BeamNG.drive only offers singleplayer, BeamMP enables driving, crashing and exploring together with friends on dedicated servers.

At the core of BeamMP is BeamNG's realistic vehicle and damage simulation. Players can drive together across expansive maps, organize races, try out stunts or simply experience the depth of the soft-body physics in multiplayer. BeamMP synchronizes vehicles and their damage between all connected players.

A BeamMP server supports multiple players at once and offers extensive customization via Lua plugins and server mods. Administrators can include custom maps, set rules and adapt the experience to their community.

BeamMP is aimed at vehicle enthusiasts and fans of physics-based simulations who want to enjoy BeamNG's detailed driving behavior together with friends. BeamMP is available on PC and requires a BeamNG.drive installation via Steam.
