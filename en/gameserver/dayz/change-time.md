# How to Change the Time on Your DayZ Server

You can adjust the speed of in-game time and the brightness of nights via the dashboard.

1. <b>Open dashboard</b><br>
   Open the dashboard of your server.

2. <b>Open settings</b><br>
   Navigate to the **Settings**.

3. <b>Adjust time multiplier</b><br>
   Enter the desired value in the **Time Multiplier** field (range: `0` - `24`).

   :::: tip Example
   A value of `12` means that a full 24-hour day in the game runs **12 times faster**, taking only 2 hours of real time.
   ::::

4. <b>Set darker nights (optional)</b><br>
   Enable the **Darker Nights** option to get more realistic and darker nights. Players will then need a light source to orient themselves.

5. <b>Restart the server</b><br>
   Save the settings and restart your server.
