# How to Enable or Disable the Third-Person View on Your DayZ Server

You can enable or disable the third-person view via the dashboard.

1. <b>Open dashboard</b><br>
   Open the dashboard of your server.

2. <b>Open settings</b><br>
   Navigate to the **Settings**.

3. <b>Toggle third person</b><br>
   Enable or disable the **Disable Third Person** option:

   - **Enabled**: Players can only use first-person view
   - **Disabled**: Players can switch between first- and third-person view

4. <b>Restart the server</b><br>
   Save the setting and restart your server.

:::: tip Tip
First-person only servers (hardcore mode) are considered more realistic and challenging, as players cannot peek around corners.
::::
