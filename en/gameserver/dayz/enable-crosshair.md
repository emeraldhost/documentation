# How to Enable or Disable the Crosshair on Your DayZ Server

You can enable or disable the crosshair via the dashboard.

1. <b>Open dashboard</b><br>
   Open the dashboard of your server.

2. <b>Open settings</b><br>
   Navigate to the **Settings**.

3. <b>Toggle crosshair</b><br>
   Enable or disable the **Disable Crosshair** option:

   - **Enabled**: Players have no crosshair and must use iron sights or scopes
   - **Disabled**: Players have a crosshair on screen

4. <b>Restart the server</b><br>
   Save the setting and restart your server.

:::: tip Tip
Servers without a crosshair are considered more realistic and are often combined with first-person only servers.
::::
