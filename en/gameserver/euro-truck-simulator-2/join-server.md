# How to Join Your Euro Truck Simulator 2 Server

## Find connection details

:::: info Note
You need the **Search ID** and the **server password** of your server. The **Search ID** is displayed in the **server console** once the server has started. The **server password** can be found in the **dashboard** of your server.
::::

## Via Convoy search

1. <b>Start Euro Truck Simulator 2</b><br>
   Launch Euro Truck Simulator 2.

2. <b>Open Convoy</b><br>
   Click on **Convoy** in the main menu.

3. <b>Search for server</b><br>
   Select **Search Convoy** and enter the **Search ID** of your server.

4. <b>Enter password</b><br>
   Enter the **server password**.

5. <b>Connect</b><br>
   Click **Join** to connect to the server.
