# How to Kick and Ban Players on Your Euro Truck Simulator 2 Server

You can kick players from your session using the in-game convoy menu.

## Kick a player

1. <b>Open the convoy menu</b><br>
   Open the convoy menu while playing on the server.

2. <b>Select the player</b><br>
   Select the player you want to kick from the player list.

3. <b>Kick the player</b><br>
   Click the option to kick the player. The player will be removed from the session.

:::: info Note
Kicked players can rejoin the session. A permanent ban feature is not available in Euro Truck Simulator 2.
::::
