# Conan Exiles

## What is Conan Exiles?

Conan Exiles is an open-world survival game developed by Funcom, set in the universe of Conan the Barbarian. You start as an exile in a brutal desert landscape and must fight to survive.

The game offers extensive building and crafting systems that allow you to construct everything from simple huts to mighty fortresses. You can capture thralls and put them to work, fight against gigantic bosses, and explore the secrets of ancient civilizations.

Conan Exiles supports both single-player and multiplayer modes and has an active modding community. The game is available on PC, PlayStation, and Xbox.
