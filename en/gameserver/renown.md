# Renown

## What is Renown?

Renown is a medieval multiplayer survival shooter focused on building, sieging and combat in a vast open world. Players explore, gather resources, build fortresses and fight alongside or against others.

At the heart of Renown are its medieval battles — from quick skirmishes between small groups to large-scale sieges with siege weapons, trebuchets and entire armies. Swordplay, archery and mounted combat are key parts of the gameplay.

Players can build their own fortresses and camps, mine resources, forge weapons and claim territory for their guild. The game features a persistent world where alliances, betrayal and control over strategic points shape the gameplay.

Renown is available on PC via Steam and is aimed at players looking for medieval PvP and PvE experiences in an open world.
