# How to Force Team Balance on Your Squad 44 Server

With this setting the server automatically ensures that both teams are filled evenly.

1. <b>Open dashboard</b><br>
   Open the dashboard of your server.

2. <b>Open settings</b><br>
   Navigate to the **Settings**.

3. <b>Adjust Force Team Balance</b><br>
   Set the **Force Team Balance** field to the desired value:
   - `true` — force team balance is enabled
   - `false` — force team balance is disabled

4. <b>Restart the server</b><br>
   Save the setting and restart your server.
