# How to Prevent Team Change on Your Squad 44 Server

You can prevent players from changing teams once they have joined a side.

1. <b>Open dashboard</b><br>
   Open the dashboard of your server.

2. <b>Open settings</b><br>
   Navigate to the **Settings**.

3. <b>Adjust Prevent Team Change</b><br>
   Set the **Prevent Team Change** field to the desired value:
   - `true` — prevent team change is enabled
   - `false` — prevent team change is disabled

4. <b>Restart the server</b><br>
   Save the setting and restart your server.
