# How to Allow Team Change on Your Squad 44 Server

You can decide whether players are allowed to change teams during a match.

1. <b>Open dashboard</b><br>
   Open the dashboard of your server.

2. <b>Open settings</b><br>
   Navigate to the **Settings**.

3. <b>Adjust Allow Team Change</b><br>
   Set the **Allow Team Change** field to the desired value:
   - `true` — team change is enabled
   - `false` — team change is disabled

4. <b>Restart the server</b><br>
   Save the setting and restart your server.
