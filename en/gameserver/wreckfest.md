# Wreckfest

## What is Wreckfest?

Wreckfest is a demolition derby racing game developed by Bugbear Entertainment and published by THQ Nordic. The game combines classic racing with aggressive crash gameplay — whoever crosses the finish line first or is the last one standing wins.

In Wreckfest, players race on dirt tracks, asphalt circuits and in arenas where the goal is to be the last car remaining. The damage model is one of the most detailed in the genre: bodies deform, parts fly off and vehicles fall apart piece by piece. Players can customize their vehicles with numerous tuning and visual parts.

On a dedicated server, up to 24 players can compete in various game modes — from classic races and last-man-standing derbies to team-based modes. Custom tracks, mods and custom game modes extend the experience almost limitlessly.

Wreckfest is available on PC via Steam and is aimed at players looking for an arcade-style, physics-based racing game with an uncompromising focus on crashes.
