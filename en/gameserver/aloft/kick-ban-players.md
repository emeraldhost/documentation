# How to Kick and Ban Players on Your Aloft Server

You can kick and ban players using the in-game admin menu.

:::: warning Requirement
You must be an admin on your server. Here you can find a guide on how to [add an admin](add-admin.md).
::::

## Kick or ban players via the admin menu

1. <b>Join the server</b><br>
   Join your Aloft server.

2. <b>Open the admin menu</b><br>
   Open the in-game admin menu.

3. <b>Select a player</b><br>
   Select the player you want to kick or ban.

4. <b>Kick or ban the player</b><br>
   Click **Kick** or **Ban** to remove the player.

:::: info Note
A kicked player can rejoin the server immediately. A banned player is permanently excluded from the server.
::::
