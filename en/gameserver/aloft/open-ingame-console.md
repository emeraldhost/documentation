# How to Open the In-Game Console in Aloft

1. <b>Start Aloft</b><br>
   Launch the game.

2. <b>Join server</b><br>
   Join your Aloft server.

3. <b>Open console</b><br>
   Press the `F1` key to open the in-game console.
