# Soulmask

## What is Soulmask?

Soulmask is a massive multiplayer survival crafting game developed by CampFire Studio. Set in a mysterious world inspired by ancient civilizations, players awaken wearing a strange and powerful mask that grants unique abilities. The game combines survival mechanics with deep crafting, base building and tribe management systems.

Players can build and expand their own tribe by recruiting tribesmen, assigning them tasks and automating workflows. Each tribesman has individual skills and traits, making strategic management essential for a thriving settlement. From gathering resources and crafting tools to defending against hostile creatures and rival tribes, Soulmask offers a rich and immersive survival experience.

The game supports up to 70 players on a single server, allowing for large-scale cooperative and competitive gameplay. Soulmask is available on PC via Steam.
