# SCP: Secret Laboratory

## What is SCP: Secret Laboratory?

SCP: Secret Laboratory is a free multiplayer horror game developed by the community team Northwood Studios. Based on the well-known SCP Foundation universe, the game drops players into a dark research facility where dangerous anomalies are contained. A breach of these entities triggers a tense scenario full of betrayal, cooperation and paranoia.

At the start of each round every player is assigned one of several roles: Class-D personnel try to escape, scientists look for a safe way out, the Mobile Task Force (MTF) attempts to retake the facility, Chaos Insurgency fights against the Foundation, and a few players take on the role of the SCPs themselves – with supernatural abilities and unique win conditions.

The facility is divided into Light Containment, Heavy Containment, Entrance Zone and the Surface. Every round offers dynamic events, shifting team dynamics and surprising twists. The game supports large lobbies and thrives on the interaction of many players on public or private servers.

SCP: Secret Laboratory is aimed at players who enjoy atmospheric horror, role-playing elements and hectic multiplayer rounds. It is available for free on PC via Steam.
