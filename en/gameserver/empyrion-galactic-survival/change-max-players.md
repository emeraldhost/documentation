# How to Change the Max Players of Your Empyrion Server

You can set how many players can play on your server at the same time.

1. <b>Open dashboard</b><br>
   Open the dashboard of your server.

2. <b>Open settings</b><br>
   Navigate to the **Settings**.

3. <b>Change max players</b><br>
   Enter the desired value in the **Max Players** field and save the setting.

4. <b>Restart the server</b><br>
   Restart your server for the change to take effect.
