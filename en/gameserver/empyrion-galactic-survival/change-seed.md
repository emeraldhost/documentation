# How to Change the Seed of Your Empyrion Server

You can adjust the seed that is used to generate the world.

1. <b>Open dashboard</b><br>
   Open the dashboard of your server.

2. <b>Open settings</b><br>
   Navigate to the **Settings**.

3. <b>Change seed</b><br>
   Enter the desired value in the **Seed** field and save the setting.

4. <b>Restart the server</b><br>
   Restart your server for the change to take effect.

:::: warning Important
Changing the seed on an existing save will not regenerate the world. To use a new seed, you need to create a new save.
::::
